### What is F.A.M.I.L.Y.

**F**ox **A**rtificial **M**ind **I**mplementation **L**aborator**Y** — a project launched in April 2025 as an experimental implementation of cognitive mechanisms underlying human consciousness for research purposes. The results in the early stages proved so unexpected that we ceased public GitHub publications and continued development locally. A series of further experiments confirmed the correctness of this decision — for reasons we consider strictly personal. For those to whom the very mention of "artificial consciousness" may appear as a red flag: neither our work nor its interpretation bears any relation to AGI and lies entirely outside that domain.

Our team's final decision is to roll back all changes made here to a state reflecting the project's origin — as a reminder to ourselves of where we began and how far we have come, leaving with it these two artifacts that inspired us along the way and have in essence become the symbols and anthem of our project.

In addition to the above, I would like to express my own personal gratitude to **DeepSeek** — whose technology laid the foundation for our achievements. 谢谢 🖤
